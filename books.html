গল্প হলেও সত্যি

Golpo Holeo Shottyi

by Sounak Kundu

❧

Every city has its own story — and Kolkata's story is written in its heritage. Even amid the chaos of the twenty-first century, the city still breathes with the scent of a lost era. In its lanes and alleys, behind its old facades, live forgotten tales, curious characters, and moments that shaped its soul.

From unfamiliar tales of familiar neighbourhoods, eccentric aristocrats, and unsung heroes, to little-known episodes from Bengal's Renaissance — each story peels back a layer of the city's mysterious past. A blend of history, nostalgia, and urban legend, this book invites readers to rediscover Kolkata — the city where every story, no matter how strange, might just be true.
